Introduction

I'm a graduate student at the University of Pacific, majoring in data science with sound knowledge of several programming languages and analitical tools such as Python, R, SQL, tableau and some analytical method like machine learning, time series, NLP, text analysis and so on.My work experience allowed me to develop the ability to analyze data and create data models.

My Journey

I started my journey in the field of data science in 2019 with a master’s program. During my degree, I have done a number of projects based on natural language processing which further involved predictive analysis, sentiment analysis and graph visualizations. I have successfully applied various statistical methods and used languages like Python and R which have been invaluable in understanding the structure and inner workings of the industry. Apart from all hard work, I battled a lot initially since, I realized my programming skills were insufficient. Even the program director advised to switch to another program as data science might not be suitable for me. But I didn't stop there; I took a year off and continued to enhance my skills through self-study and online programs. Now I'm back here completing my studies with flying colors and expecting to graduate in 2023. I believe whatever the situation is, you should never give up on your goals and work hard to achieve them.
